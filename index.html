A Symphony of Two

We are a small, seasoned duo with 5+ years of experience!

Dan Caspers

Developer by Day, Keyboardist by Night

Dan is working towards a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the University of Minnesota with an emphasis in Human Computer Interaction. He hopes to find a way to tie his love of computing technologies and music by putting his talent into a company that makes a positive impact in the lives of musicians. His musical background lacks the pedigree of many keyboardists, but his vast assortment of lesser known influences has led to a style and sound that his colleagues have called "truly one of a kind".

Gordon Hayes

Saxophonist, Composer and Arranger

Gordon is working towards a Bachelor of Music in Jazz Studies under Ryan Frane and Adam Booker, in addition to studying reed instruments with Theodore Schoen. His musical vision is divided between trying to develop a personal music aesthetic and trying to revive the sounds of the masters who inspired his career. An avid listener as well as performer, he's studied the sounds of recording giants like Stan Getz, Dexter Gordon, Chet Baker, and Miles Davis to breathe character and soul into his sound. He has also worked outside of the realm of jazz with local professionals like Zeitgeist as well as performing gospel and pop music in the annual UMD Vocal Jazz Cabaret. This flexibility, the fusion of experimentation and deep rooted tradition, helps elevates Like a Bossa from a cover band to something truly unique.

Like a Bossa formed back in our high school years. Most of our performances then were unpaid opportunities to bring live music into the community. We performed at celebrations in our hometown and events at the high school, but the highlight of those years was a concert for disadvantaged young students; some of them had never heard jazz music or even seen a live performance before. After a couple of weeks of classroom visits to motivate the students to join the school band, we took to the auditorium stage to help inspire the next generation of musicians. We managed to hold the attention of kids between ages 6-11 for around an hour, putting a spin on music ranging from Disney classics to Chic Corea and Stevie Wonder while also giving them plenty to watch. With Dan playing two pianos at a time and Gordon captivating the audience by dancing around and playing his familiar melodies, we were able to bring joy to those children in a way unlike anything we had seen before.

Although college has complicated rehearsing, our song selection is ever growing. Our combined musical taste promotes sampling from numerous genres and is delightful for listeners of all ages. No matter what your event is, we hope to help you make it truly remarkable! --Like a Bossa
